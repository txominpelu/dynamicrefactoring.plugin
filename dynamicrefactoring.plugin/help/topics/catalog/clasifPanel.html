Classification Panel

The Classification Panel is located in the left part of the view and it's goal is to show the catalog of refactorings. It allows to visualize the refactorings classified by the categories of the selected classification. On top of that, it allows to apply filters to hide those refactorings who doesn't fulfill a list of established requirements.

In the following picture you can see the panel:

 


Classification Panel

 

In the upper part it has a combo to select a classification. Right under that combo there's the description of the selected classification and a tree with every refactoring in the catalog classified by the categories of the selected classification and according to the applied filters.

Under the tree, there's a list of all the existing filters with the possibily of disabling and removing each one of them and above the list there's a checkbox that allows to hide or show the filtered refactorings.

In the upper right corner it's located the filtering field and the button to apply the new filters. For more information on how to create the filters they are explained in the filtering section.

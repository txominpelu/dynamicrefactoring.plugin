Refactorings catalog

Refactoring Catalog Browser's view shows a catalog of all available refactorings. On top of that it shows all the information related with every refactoring, allows to visualize refactorings according to different classifications offering a wide range of filtering options.

This view is composed ofthree different parts:

  • The toolbar called Refactoring Catalog Browser Toolbar.

  • A classification panel that shows the refactorings classified.

  • A summary panel that shows all information available about the selected refactoring.
